Location

Location / Directions to ACCESS-MEED Department

The MEED Program administrative office is conveniently located in the heart of downtown Miami at street-level between transportation drop-off points and steps from rail transportation at Miami Dade College Wolfson Campus.
Our address is:

300 N.E. 2nd Avenue, Room 1181
Miami, FL 33132

We welcome visitors to our fully accessible facilities. We are open Monday through Friday, 8:30 A.M. to 4:30 P.M.

To arrange a visit please call our receptionist at (305)237-3397.

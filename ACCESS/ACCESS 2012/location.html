Miami Dade College Wolfson Campus

Location / Map

The ACCESS - MEED Program administrative office is conveniently located in the heart of downtown Miami at street-level between transportation drop-off points and steps from rail transportation at Miami Dade College Wolfson Campus. We welcome visitors to our fully accessible facilities. We are open Monday through Thursday, 7:30 A.M. to 6:00 P.M. and Friday 7:30 a.m. - 4:30 p.m. If you would like to arrange a visit please call our receptionist at (305) 237-3397.

Address:

300 N.E. 2nd Avenue, Room 1180
Miami, FL 33132

Map

Click this link to view larger map and for directions

By Metrorail:

  • Exit Metrorail at Government Center and take the Metromover (Inner or Outer Loop) to College/Bayside Station or College North Station.

From the North:

  • Take I-95 South and exit right onto NW 8th Street.
  • Go straight to 5th Street.
  • Go seven blocks to NE 2nd Ave.

From the South:

  • Take US 1 to I-95 North and exit right onto NW 2nd Street.
  • Go north two blocks to NW 5th Street and turn right.
  • Go east six blocks to NE 2nd Avenue and turn right.

From the East:

  • Take MacArthur Causeway West and exit onto Biscayne Boulevard.
  • Go south eight blocks and turn right on NE 3rd Street.

From the West:

  • Take SR 836 East and exit onto I-395.
  • Exit right onto NE 2nd Avenue.
  • Go south eight blocks.

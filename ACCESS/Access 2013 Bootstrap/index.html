  • About ACCESS

    ACCESS is the Wolfson Campus' disability service center staffed with six Resource Advisors who are specially trained to guide students through the registration process and develop a unique set of accommodations and services for each student that is built upon their strengths and abilities.

  • Contact

    Contact our staff for assistance. Advisors, technical staff, tutors, and more

  • Join our student organization

    Find how you can help others as you improve your own performance.

  • Are you eligible for our services ?

    Students who have a documented disability are eligible for services. To learn more on how you're eligible for service please make an appointment with your advisor today.

  • Mission

    The mission of ACCESS is to assist students to maximize their talents, skills, and abilities to improve academic success as we also promote awareness of accessible and inclusive options whenever possible in classroom learning and in every part of the college experience at the Wolfson Campus.

  • Berger Gallery

    The William Berger Gallery is a community access to the Arts, which nurtures and celebrates the creativity of people with disabilities through shared experiences in the visual and performing arts.

  • MEED DIGITAL TECH STUDIO

    MEED Digital Tech Studio is equipped with the latest in Apple technology and software that has integrated universal access features. Videos and alternative format materials that are accessible are developed by students who are getting training, service, and work experience as they transition to employment.

  • The MEED Program

    The MEED Program is a college-wide program in employment assistance for MDC students with disabilities.

About us

This website is sponsored by the Association of Agencies (AOA), a network of non-profit and government-funded agencies and educational institutions in South Florida that meet each month and work together to address the needs of diverse populations including persons with disabilities, veterans, seniors, and many others. One of our primary goals is to find or develop new opportunities for employment, educational training and experiences, community service inclusion and much more.

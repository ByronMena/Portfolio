Living AOA

Resiliency Campaign

Sponsored by the American Red Cross, this Campaign helps prepare the community to know how to prepare and recover in emergency situations.

Emergency Preparedness Outreach

The AOA members researched and created an accessible booklet for the elderly and persons with disabilities located in South Florida. It is Designed to help these individuals make basic plans for emergencies, prepare resources for emergencies, and know who to contact for help. This booklet is available along with an important info sheet and envelope to be placed on a refrigerator and readied to take along when an emergency causes an evacuation.

  • How it all started.

    Some people already know what they want to do in life since they day they were in grade school, for me it didn't start out that way. I was born deaf and so with that I had a hard time deciding what I wanted to do with my life. I decided to try out being a chef so I went to Johnson & Wales University. It was during my second internship at Specialty Bakery where I truly discovered my passion with cakes. Since then, I have been making unique cakes and exceeding the expectation of my customers.

  • Living the dream

    I currently working alongside with Jason who is also a cake designer who provides me his art design expertise to make my cake decoration tailored to customer's taste and dreams. We're dreaming big and growing. Give us a try and I promise you will not want anyone else make your cake.
